Our coaches are isolated older people. To visit them you will need to become verified following the steps below.

Become a member

If you're a member we’ll give you personal fitness advice, help you achieve your goals, send you one off missions, send you a new GoodGym running top and can pair you with an older person to be your coach.

Background Check

To go on missions you need to perform a background check (DBS). This involves presenting a number of documents to someone at GoodGym.

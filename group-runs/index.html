Group runs

We run in a group to a local organisation that needs help, complete a task like painting a fence or planing a tree, have a fun fitness session, and run back in 90 minutes. All fitness levels are welcome and we never leave anyone behind.

These are the steps to reaching your running goal

Step one

Run

We organise weekly group runs which are like flash mobs for good

Step two

Help

We run to community organisations and help them with tasks such as gardening or renovating.

Step three

Run

We like to have fun and run home. Sometimes treating ourselves to a lemonade in the local pub.

Step four

Stretch

Looking after you is also important to us. We have a certified trainer who leads the runs and makes sure you reach your goals
